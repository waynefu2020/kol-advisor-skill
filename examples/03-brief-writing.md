# Example: Brief Writing

**User prompt:**
> /kol-advisor Write a brief for a campaign. Product: wireless noise-canceling earphones ($79). Target: college students in the US. Working with 10 nano/KOC creators (5K–30K followers). Goal: brand awareness + TikTok views.

**Wayne's response:**
For nano creators, go stricter — give them a reference video and let them replicate the style. Here's the brief:

---

**[Brand Name] x [Creator Name] — TikTok Collab Brief**

**Brand Tone**
[Brand Name] makes premium audio gear that doesn't cost a fortune. Our earphones are built for students who need to focus — in the library, on the commute, or in a loud dorm. Clean design, serious sound.

**Product Info**
- Product: [Product Name] Wireless Noise-Canceling Earphones
- Price: $79
- Why people use it: Block out distractions and get into focus mode anywhere

**Campaign Purpose**
Awareness — get views, get people talking about the product

**Deliverables**
- Format: Dedicated TikTok video (talking head / lifestyle)
- Length: 40–60 seconds
- Revisions: Max 2 rounds, minor adjustments only
- Submit draft: 7 days before publish date
- Publish by: [DATE]

**Key Ideas (pick max 3, stay focused)**
1. Real noise cancellation — not just "decent" — you stop hearing the world
2. $79 for this quality is genuinely surprising
3. Built for studying / campus life

**Reference Videos**
Please watch these before filming — match the energy and structure:
- [Link 1]
- [Link 2]

**Caption**
Include: [Brand Name], #noisecanceling #studytok #collegelife #focus

**Payment**
- 50% on draft approval, 50% within 3 days of publish
- Method: PayPal

**Content Rights**
We may repost your video on our brand account and use it in paid promotion for 12 months.

---

**Wayne's note on this brief:** For nano creators, the reference videos do most of the work. Don't over-explain — they'll replicate what they see. Keep Key Ideas to 3 max; if you list 5, they'll try to fit everything in and the video will feel forced. Max 2 revision rounds — any more and the relationship sours fast.
